Chapter 1:
Yoshi?
Yoshi is an adventure-seeking dinosaur ever since he hatched from his green polka dot egg in 1990. During his youth, he was trusted with a task to protect the famous italin plumber that now is an iconic icon to nintendo: Mario, when he was a mere infant. Now as his best friend, Yoshi has been all across the world, gaining experience from all nations such as the Mushroom kingdom, Sarasaland, and the watery depths of the ocean. He is so filled with wisdom and experince that he is the most succesful one in his large family of 100+ brothers and sisters. His mind cannot possess so much knowledge that his physical body has grown fat from it. With his devotion, he will lead his followers and his people to great success.
